✏️ Section A: Multiple Choice (5 marks)

Circle the correct answer.

  1. Which part of the story grabs attention at the start?

    a) Funny part b) Most exciting moment c) The ending d) The beginning
  3. In a narrative, pacing refers to:
    a) Number of characters b) Speed of events c) Setting d) Dialogue
  4. A resolution is:
    a) Where the conflict starts b) How the problem is solved c) Prediction d) Middle of story
  5. Which of these is an example of a conflict?
    a) “The sun was shining.” b) “Sipho realised he forgot his homework.” c) “The story ended happily.” d) “They introduced the characters.”
